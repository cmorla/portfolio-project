Our Work

At AI of Color our mission is to equip impacted communities with the tools and information required to understand and question unfair practices resulting from algorithmic and machine learning techniques. We will also work to promote diversity and inclusion in the technical and policy dialogues relating to artificial intelligence.

THE PATH TOWARD NON-DISCRIMINATORY AI SYSTEMS

Artificial intelligence (AI) is at a turning point where progress is contingent upon building fair, transparent, and accountable systems from the outset. This involves awareness and education, technical solutions, and stakeholder commitment. The broad ramifications of the issue require a multidisciplinary approach and joint efforts.
